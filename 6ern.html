Cod warzone tips and tricks

Perk list 1

Perk 1 is named Doubble Time. It doubles the duration of Tactical Sprint. Increase crouch movement speed by 30%

Perk 2 is named E.O.D. Take reduced damage from non-killstreak explosives and fire.

Perk 3 is named Scavenger. Resupply ammo from dead players.

Perk 4 is named Cold-Blooded. Undetectable by AI targeting system and thermal optics. Does not trigger High Alert warning.

Perk 5 is named Kill chain. Increases your chance of finding killstreaks in supply boxes. No effect in Warzone Rumble.

Perk 6 is named Quick fix . Killing players immediately starts health regeneration. Capturing and holding objectives increases your health regeneration rate.

Perk list 2

Perk 1 is named Restock. Recharge equipment over 50 second

Perk 2 is named hardline. 25% off kiosk prices for killstreaks, field upgrades, and armor plates. No effect in Warzone Rumble.

Perk 3 is named overkill. Carry Two primary weapons, cannot equip two of the same weapon and secondaries cannot be equipped.

Perk 4 is named High alert. Your vision pulses when enemies outside of your view see you.

Perk 5 is named ghost. Undetectable by UAVs, Radar Drones, and Heartbeat Sensors.

Perk 6 is named pointman. All members of your team earn more money from completed missions. No effect in Warzone Rumble

Perk list 3

Perk 1 is named Tune up .Reduce the charge time of field upgrades by 40% and reduce revive time by 25%.

Perk 2 is named Amped. Faster weapon swap and rocket launcher reload speed.

Perk 3 is named Sharpnel. Spawn with an extra piece of lethal equipment. Explosive damage delays enemy health regeneration.

Perk 4 is named Battle Hardened. Reduce strength of enemy flash, stun, EMP, and gas effects. Immune to Snapshot Grenades.

Perk 5 is named Spotter. See enemy equipment, field upgrades, and killstreaks through walls. Mark them for your team by aiming down sights. Hack enemy Claymores, Proximity Mines, C4, and Trophy Systems.

Perk 6 is named Tracker. Enemies leave behind a footprint trail. See markers at enemy death locations, and hide the death markers of enemy you kill.
